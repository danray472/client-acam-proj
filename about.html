About Africa College of Aviation and Management

Africa College of Aviation and Management is a professional technical and management organization committed to the promotion of excellence and integrity in the practice of both technical and business courses. The college was established in 2012 as a supreme institute that provides aviation and business services to enhance equitable knowledge-gain within and outside Africa.

Our History

Africa College of Aviation and Management was established in 2011 and was first registered by the Ministry of Education, Science, and Technology in Kenya in 2012 to offer Diploma, Certificate, and professional courses in diverse fields. We adopt modern, friendly, and state-of-the-art delivery systems across the college. Our student enrollment cuts across all religions, cultures from Kenya, and across the continent and beyond. All these students undergo rigorous training in their respective fields and receive well-deserved international exposure.
